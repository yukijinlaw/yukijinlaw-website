FAQ

Answers to common questions before you reach out.

Every matter is fact-specific. These answers are general information, not legal advice.

What types of clients does Yuki Jin Law work with?

The firm works with California businesses, founders, professionals, and individuals facing business-related legal questions or disputes.

Do you handle both litigation and contract matters?

Yes. The practice includes commercial litigation, dispute strategy, contract drafting, contract review, and business counseling.

Can I get help before a dispute becomes a lawsuit?

Yes. Early legal guidance can help preserve evidence, assess leverage, identify business options, and avoid unnecessary escalation.

How does the consultation process work?

Start with a brief inquiry. If the matter appears appropriate for the firm, a consultation can be scheduled to review the facts, goals, urgency, and next steps.

Does contacting the firm create an attorney-client relationship?

No. An attorney-client relationship is formed only after the firm confirms it can represent you and both sides sign an engagement agreement.
