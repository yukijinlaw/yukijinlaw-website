Professional biography

Trusted by clients who value clarity, discretion, and measured advocacy.

Yuki Jin is a California attorney whose practice is shaped by a deep understanding of business dynamics and a disciplined approach to dispute resolution. Her work spans contract strategy, risk counseling, and negotiations that require both precision and perspective.

Before entering private practice, she developed a strong foundation in finance, analytics, and executive education through advanced study at Harvard Business School, Northwestern Kellogg, Peking University Guanghua, and a Bachelor of Finance. That background helps her translate legal issues into practical commercial decisions.

Fluent in English, Chinese, and Korean, Yuki serves clients across cultural and linguistic contexts with clear communication and thoughtful leadership.
