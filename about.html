Biography

Yuki Jin is a California attorney with a background in finance and executive business education. She advises individuals and small businesses on business and contract matters, civil litigation, personal injury, immigration-related legal support, and legal consulting.

Education includes a Juris Doctor; the Harvard Business School Business Analytics Program; Executive MBA studies at Northwestern University Kellogg and Peking University Guanghua; and a Bachelor of Finance.

Languages: English, Mandarin Chinese, and Korean.
